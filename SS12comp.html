Kith and Kin

The team behind Kith and Kin include Heather Elis, Yolina Kostova and Candice Stewart from the University of Dundee's School of Computing in Scotland, UK. They are currently in their fourth year of undergraduate studies in BSc Applied Computing. With an interest in Human Computer Interaction (HCI) and Accessibility, they strive to improve their applications to ensure they develop inclusive systems.
